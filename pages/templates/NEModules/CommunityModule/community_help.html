HOW TO USE THE DISCUSSIONS MODULE

Press the Add discussion button if you want to start a new discussion. Fill the title and message and press "Save"
Click one of the items in the discussions list panel to view discussion details.
When you are in one of the discussion details click the Add reply button to add a new reply on the discussion. Enter your message and click save to submit your reply.
Press the Help button to view this help content.
